# Content Integrity Policy / 内容诚信政策

## English

### Our Commitment to Honest Content

We are committed to providing accurate, honest, and transparent information to our users. This policy ensures that all content on our website maintains the highest standards of integrity.

### What We DON'T Do

❌ **NO Fake Statistics**
- No invented download numbers
- No fabricated user ratings
- No made-up accuracy percentages
- No false testimonials

❌ **NO Misleading Pricing**
- No specific prices for unreleased products
- No fake "original price" comparisons
- No misleading "limited time" offers

❌ **NO False Claims**
- No exaggerated capabilities
- No unverified performance claims
- No misleading comparisons

### What We DO

✅ **Honest Product Status**
- Clearly state if a product is in development
- Indicate beta/alpha status when applicable
- Be transparent about limitations

✅ **Accurate Information**
- Use general descriptions instead of specific fake numbers
- Cite sources when making claims
- Update content when information changes

✅ **Transparent Communication**
- Clearly distinguish between released and unreleased features
- Indicate when information is estimated or projected
- Admit when we don't have specific data

### Content Guidelines

#### For Unreleased Products:
- Use phrases like "coming soon", "in development", "planned features"
- Avoid specific numbers (downloads, ratings, users)
- Don't mention pricing until officially determined

#### For Released Products:
- Use actual, verifiable statistics
- Link to official sources (app stores, reviews)
- Update regularly to maintain accuracy

#### For Comparisons:
- Use general terms ("popular", "widely-used") instead of fake numbers
- Focus on features rather than invented metrics
- Be fair and balanced

### Review Process

All content should be reviewed to ensure:
1. No fake statistics or numbers
2. No misleading claims
3. Clear indication of product status
4. Accurate and up-to-date information

## 中文

### 我们对诚实内容的承诺

我们致力于为用户提供准确、诚实和透明的信息。本政策确保我们网站上的所有内容保持最高的诚信标准。

### 我们不做的事

❌ **不编造统计数据**
- 不虚构下载量
- 不编造用户评分
- 不捏造准确率百分比
- 不伪造用户评价

❌ **不误导定价**
- 未发布产品不标注具体价格
- 不虚假"原价"对比
- 不误导性"限时"优惠

❌ **不虚假宣传**
- 不夸大功能
- 不发布未经验证的性能声明
- 不误导性比较

### 我们做的事

✅ **诚实的产品状态**
- 明确说明产品是否在开发中
- 适当时标注测试版/内测版状态
- 对限制保持透明

✅ **准确的信息**
- 使用一般性描述而非具体虚假数字
- 发表声明时引用来源
- 信息变化时及时更新内容

✅ **透明的沟通**
- 明确区分已发布和未发布功能
- 说明信息是估计或预测时要标注
- 没有具体数据时坦诚承认

### 内容指南

#### 对于未发布产品：
- 使用"即将推出"、"开发中"、"计划功能"等措辞
- 避免具体数字（下载量、评分、用户数）
- 在正式确定前不提及价格

#### 对于已发布产品：
- 使用实际的、可验证的统计数据
- 链接到官方来源（应用商店、评论）
- 定期更新以保持准确性

#### 对于比较：
- 使用一般性术语（"流行的"、"广泛使用的"）而非虚假数字
- 关注功能而非虚构的指标
- 保持公平和平衡

### 审查流程

所有内容都应审查以确保：
1. 没有虚假统计或数字
2. 没有误导性声明
3. 清楚标明产品状态
4. 信息准确且最新
